To My Love

My love, I don’t know if words can truly convey how deeply sorry I am for the pain I’ve caused you. Asking for a breakup was not just a mistake—it was a reflection of my own insecurities, not a lack of love. In truth, my feelings for you run so deep that the thought of losing you terrifies me. You are my heart, my safe haven, and the greatest gift I have ever been blessed with. I acted out of fear, not reason, and for that, I am profoundly sorry.

You are the light in my life, the one who makes every moment brighter, even when the world feels heavy. I can’t imagine a future without you by my side because you are my everything. Your thoughts, your feelings, your dreams—they mean more to me than I could ever express. I know I’ve hurt you, but I need you to know that no matter how hard life gets, you are the only one I will ever want.

I love you with all my heart and soul, and I hate that my actions have made you question that. Please believe me when I say that I am ready to do whatever it takes to prove my love to you. You are the one who completes me, the one I want to cherish forever. Forgive me for my weakness and let me show you how deeply, endlessly, and unconditionally I love you.
